How to Use:

  1. Set Paper Size: Choose width and height for your maze
  2. Place Waypoints: Click on the canvas to place points - the track appears automatically!
  3. Adjust: Use "Undo Last Point" to remove the last waypoint, or "Clear All" to start over
  4. Track Width: Adjust the slider to make your track wider or narrower - updates in real-time
  5. Print: When satisfied, click "Print Maze"

Tips:

  • First point = START, Last point = FINISH
  • The track updates automatically as you add waypoints
  • Place waypoints strategically to create curves, zigzags, and interesting paths
  • Use more waypoints for complex mazes, fewer for simple ones
  • Try vertical layouts (1 wide x 2+ tall) for challenging long mazes
