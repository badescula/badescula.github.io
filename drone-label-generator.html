📋 Instructions & QR Code Behavior

Operator ID: Required. Displays on both label and in QR code.

Phone Number: Optional. Only in QR code, not on label.

When scanned:

• With phone: Phone offers to save as contact (name: "Drone Operator", phone, operator ID in notes)

• Without phone: Shows operator ID as text you can copy

QR Size: 20mm works for ID only, 25mm+ recommended with phone number.

Printing: Single A4 page. Use sticker paper at 100% scale.

TEST: Always test with your phone camera before printing.
